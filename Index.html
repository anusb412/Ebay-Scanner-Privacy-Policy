Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Grail Scout ("App," "we," "us," "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices, including what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using Grail Scout, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Camera and Image Data

When you use the scanning feature, we temporarily capture images from your device camera to:

  • Detect product labels and text via vision AI
  • Extract item information (brand, model, condition)
  • Generate price estimates using OpenAI's GPT-4 vision model

Important: Images are processed in real-time on our secure backend servers and are not stored, retained, or logged after processing completes. We do not build a database of your photos.

2.2 Search Queries

When you search for items or enter product titles, we send this text to:

  • eBay API: To find comparable sold listings and pricing data
  • OpenAI API: To generate market estimates and valuation insights

These queries are processed securely and are not stored for training or further analysis.

2.3 Device Information

We may collect limited device information for app functionality and debugging:

  • Device OS version and model (for compatibility)
  • App version and crash logs (for error reporting)
  • Approximate location (if you enable it; used for local market insights)

2.4 Analytics and Usage Data

We track basic usage patterns to improve the app:

  • Number of searches performed
  • Features used (scanner, text search, price estimation)
  • App session duration

This data is aggregated and anonymized; we cannot identify individual users.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core app services: Scanning, searching, and price estimation
  • To improve the app: Analyzing usage patterns and fixing bugs
  • To communicate: Sending important updates or security notices
  • To comply with law: Responding to legal requests or protecting rights

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 eBay API

Grail Scout integrates with eBay's Finding API to retrieve sold item listings and pricing data. When you search, your query is sent to eBay's servers. eBay's privacy practices govern how they handle this data.

eBay Privacy Policy: https://www.ebay.com/help/policies/member-behaviour-policies/ebay-privacy-policy

4.2 OpenAI API

We use OpenAI's GPT-4 Vision and GPT-4 Mini models for image analysis and market estimation. When you submit an image or search query:

  • Images are sent to OpenAI for vision processing
  • Images are deleted after processing (not retained for training)
  • You can review OpenAI's data policies for details

OpenAI Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
OpenAI API Data Privacy: https://openai.com/policies/api-data-usage-policies

4.3 Other APIs

The app may use third-party services for barcode lookup and alternative scraping methods. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • HTTPS encryption: All data transmitted between your app and our servers uses industry-standard TLS encryption
  • Server-side API keys: Sensitive credentials (OpenAI, eBay) are stored securely on our backend, never embedded in the app
  • No local storage of sensitive data: Your API keys and personal identifiers are not saved locally on your device
  • Regular audits: We monitor for vulnerabilities and perform security reviews

However, no security system is perfect. Please protect your device and network access.

6. Data Retention

  • Images: Deleted immediately after processing; not stored
  • Search queries: Not logged or retained (processed in real-time only)
  • Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymized; individual records deleted after 90 days
  • Error logs: Kept for 30 days for debugging; then deleted

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Deletion

Since we don't build profiles or databases of user data, there is no personal data file to access. However, you can:

  • Request confirmation of what data we process (typically none after your session ends)
  • Ask us to delete any analytics records associated with your device

7.2 Camera and Location Permissions

You control which permissions the app can use:

  • Camera: Required for the scanning feature; you can deny this and use text search instead
  • Location: Optional; used only for local market insights if enabled

7.3 Opt-Out of Analytics

In the app settings, you can opt out of analytics tracking. This will prevent us from collecting usage data.

8. Children's Privacy

Grail Scout is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it immediately. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information should contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Our servers are hosted in the United States. If you are located outside the U.S., your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using Grail Scout, you consent to such transfers. Data may be subject to U.S. laws, including potential access by government agencies.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date below
  • Posting a notice in the app or on our website

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
